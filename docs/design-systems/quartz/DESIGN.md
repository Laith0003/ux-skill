# Quartz

Quartz is clean and calm. A crisp cool-white surface, a confident accent, clear hierarchy, and no decoration for its own sake. For SaaS, dashboards, and product UI where clarity is the brand.

## Accessibility

Every text pair meets WCAG AA, enforced at synthesis time:

- Ink on canvas: 15.48:1
- Body on canvas: 6.75:1
- Muted on canvas: 4.57:1
- Text on primary: 5.45:1
