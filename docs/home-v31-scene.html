The turn

Brief → system

Five deterministic steps, start to ship.

No catalogue to pick from. The engine reads the brief, synthesizes a novel language, anchors it to your brand, then refuses to ship anything that breaks. The card at right is real output.

  1. 01 · BRIEF

    Read the brief

    Industry, audience, the mood — parsed into a structured intake, no guesswork.

  2. 02 · SYNTHESIZE

    Set seven axes

    Warmth, contrast, density, geometry, formality, motion, type — tuned, then emitted as tokens.

  3. 03 · ANCHOR

    Anchor to your brand

    Primary pulled from your logo's pixels — not the nearest CSS named colour.

  4. 04 · LINT

    Lint 152 anti-patterns

    The centroid — gradient headers, three equal cards, filler verbs — fails the build here.

  5. 05 · VERIFY

    Verify mobile, for real

    Rendered at true 360 / 390px in headless Chrome. Horizontal scroll fails, not your users.
